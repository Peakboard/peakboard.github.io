To use the Peakboard Hub in the Peakboard Designer, you have to connect it in the Peakboard Designer.

![Peakboard Designer connection](/assets/images/hub/de_hub_connecttodesigner.gif)

### Establish connection to Peakboard Hub

1. **Open Peakboard Hub dialog:** Click the Peakboard Hub button at the top of the Peakboard Designer to open the connection window.
2. **Select connection type:** Choose the desired connection type from the dropdown menu: **OnPremise** (for local Hub installations on your own server) or **Online** (for the cloud-based Hub variant).
3. **Enter connection data:** Enter the required data: the **Peakboard Hub URL** (OnPremise only), your **username** and your **password**.
4. **Establish connection:** Click the [Connect] button to establish the connection to Peakboard Hub.
5. **Save connection:** After a successful connection, the status shows [Connected]. Click [OK] to save the settings.

After saving the connection, you can upload your projects to Peakboard Hub and use all the features that Peakboard Hub offers.
