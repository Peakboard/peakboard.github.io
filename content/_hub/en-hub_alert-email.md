In this article you will learn how to activate email alerts in Peakboard Hub.
Once set up, you will automatically receive email notifications when new alerts occur on your Peakboard Box.

![Email alerts](/assets/images/hub/de_hub_alert-email.gif)

### Set up email alerts

1. **Access alert settings:** Navigate to the [Alerts] menu item in the left sidebar. In the right panel, you will find [Alert Settings] for managing email notifications.
2. **Add email address:** Click the [Add] button in Alert Settings. Enter the desired email address in the [E-mail Address] field and click [Save].
3. **Activate notifications:** Check the checkbox next to the email address to activate notifications. You can toggle notifications on and off at any time. Multiple email addresses can be added and managed individually.
