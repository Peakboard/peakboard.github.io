To remove an existing list from Peakboard Hub, follow the steps below.
Once completed, the selected list is permanently deleted.

### Delete a list

Navigate to the [Lists] section in the left sidebar and click the three dots next to the list you want to delete. In the context menu, select [Delete] (1).

![Delete list](/assets/images/hub/delete-list/delete-list-01-menu.png)

A confirmation dialog appears. Click [Yes] to permanently delete the list, or [No] to cancel.

![Confirm deletion](/assets/images/hub/delete-list/delete-list-02-confirm.png)

After confirmation, the selected list is removed from the system and no longer appears in the list overview.
