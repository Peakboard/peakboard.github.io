To remove an existing list from Peakboard Hub, follow the steps below.
Once completed, the selected list is permanently deleted.

![Delete list](/assets/images/hub/de_hub_list-delete.gif)

### Delete a list

1. **Select list:** Navigate to the [Lists] section in the left sidebar and click the three dots next to the list you want to delete.
2. **Choose delete:** In the dropdown menu that appears, click [Delete].
3. **Confirm deletion:** A confirmation dialog appears. Click [Yes] to permanently delete the list, or [No] to cancel.

After confirmation, the selected list is removed from the system and no longer appears in the list overview.
