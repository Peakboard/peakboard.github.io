In this article you will learn how to create and upload a new flow in Peakboard Hub.

![Create flow](/assets/images/hub/de_hub_flow-create.gif)

### Create flow

1. **Start new data project:** Open Peakboard Designer and click the [New Flow project] button in the central area of the start page.
2. **Establish Peakboard Hub connection:** After creating the flow, establish a connection to Peakboard Hub by clicking the corresponding button in the upper menu bar.
3. **Confirm connection:** Fill in the required connection details and confirm with [OK].

### Edit and upload flow

1. **Create flow:** Begin creating your flow. After completion, upload the flow using the [Upload] button.
2. **Name flow:** Assign a descriptive name for your flow and click [OK].
3. **View flow in Hub:** After successful upload, find your flow in Peakboard Hub under [Flows] in the flow list.

Your new flow is now created and uploaded. The flow is available in Peakboard Hub for editing and use.
